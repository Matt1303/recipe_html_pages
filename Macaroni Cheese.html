Macaroni Cheese

Ingredients

STARTER SAUCE

600ml whole milk, at room temperature

50g unsalted butter

50g plain flour

a pinch of salt


400g macaroni/spirali

230g mature Cheddar, grated

90g Monterey Jack, grated

30g Parmesan, grated

½ tsp English mustard

30g mozzarella, grated (optional)

Directions

(Preheat the grill to 220°C/200°C (fan)/gas 7 - if adding mozzarella on top and baking for crispness)

Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil and cook the pasta according to the pack instructions, until al dente. Tip into a colander and leave to drain completely.

In a heavy-bottomed pan, make the Starter Sauce:
Warm the milk until hot but not boiling.
Melt the butter in a large pan, then reduce the heat to low and gradually stir in the flour using a wooden spoon until you have a paste - this creates a roux. Continue to cook, stirring continuously, for 3-4 minutes, or until the roux turns golden in colour, being careful not to overbrown and burn the mixture.
Add the hot milk to the roux a little at a time, stirring vigorously. Once the mixture is fully combined, turn up the heat to medium and cook for a further 5-8 minutes, or until the sauce is thick enough to coat the back of the wooden spoon. Make sure you stir continuously to keep the sauce smooth and so that it doesn't stick to the bottom of the pan.
Season with salt.

Reduce the heat to low. Add all the cheese except the mozzarella, then the mustard, and stir until melted. Stir in the drained pasta.

(OPTIONAL:
Transfer the mixture to an ovenproof dish, sprinkle over the mozzarella and place under the grill for 6-8 minutes, or until blistered and bubbling. Woof.)
