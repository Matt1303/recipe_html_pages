Super Seedy Breakfast Porridge

★★★★

Breakfasts, Fibre Fuelled

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2

Ingredients

70 grams jumbo oats

120 ml unsweetened almond milk

2 tablespoons pumpkin seeds, roughly chopped

Pinch ground ginger

Pinch ground cinnamon

Pinch ground nutmeg

2 tablespoons hemp seeds

1 tablespoon almond butter, plus more for serving

2 teaspoons chia seeds

0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract

100% maple syrup (optional)

Berries, to serve

Description

This super seedy breakfast porridge is packed with ALA-rich hemp and and chia sceds. While the ingredient list looks long, it's mostly spices and flavourings. If you have pumpkin pie spice on hand, you can use that instead of the ginger, cinnamon, and nutmeg.

For those who prefer a sweeter porridge, add maple syrup as desired. We like this best with fresh berries and a drizzle of almond butter on top.

Directions

Bring the oats and 160 ml water to the boil in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the almond milk, pumpkin seeds, ginger, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes, until the oats are tender.

Remove from the heat and stir in the hemp seeds, almond butter, chia seds, and vanilla. Taste, adding maple syrup as desired for a wetter porridge.

Serve with berries and additional almond butter drizzle, if desired.
