Chilli Mac

Dinner, Fibre Fuelled, Lunches, Part Of Other Recipe

Ingredients

Easy Cheese Sauce


30 grams cashews, soaked in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drained

0.5 orange sweet pepper, roughly chopped

120 ml unsweetened almond milk

1 heaping tablespoon nutritional yeast, plus more to taste

1 teaspoon chilli powder, plus more to taste

4 teaspoon salt, plus more to taste


Chili Mac

500 grams cooked macaroni noodles

600 grams reserved 4-Bean Chilli (separate recipe)

Directions

Place the cashews, sweet pepper, almond milk, nutritional yeast, chilli powder, and salt in the base of a food processor and puree until very creamy and smooth. Season to taste, adding in more salt, chill powder, or nutritional yeast as desired.

Cook the noodles according to the packet instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.

Warm the reserved chilli, then add in the cooked noodles and cheese sauce. Stir together and enjoy!
