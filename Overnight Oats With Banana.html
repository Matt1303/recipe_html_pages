Overnight Oats With Banana

Vegetarian Athlete's Cookbook

Servings: 1

Ingredients

40g rolled oats

75 g low fat plain Greek yogurt

125 ml any milk of your choice

1 tbsp chia seeds

pinch of cinnamon

banana, sliced

Optional toppings: Toasted flaked almonds, fresh blackberries, raspberries or strawberries

Description

This variation of overnight oats includes banana, which provides natural sweetness, help thicken the mixture. The seeds are rich in omega-3 fats, fibre and protein, and, unlike flax seeds don't have to be ground to be absorbed by the body. And the toppings are endless!

Directions

Mix together the oats, yogurt, milk, chia seeds, cinnamon and sliced banana in a bowl. Cover and put in the fridge overnight so the oats absorb all the yogurt.

In the morning, remove from the fridge. Scatter over flaked almonds and top with fresh fruit (optional). You can also add extra milk or yogurt if you like.

Nutrition

NUTRITION per serving:
418 cals
21g protein
9 fat (1g saturates)
58g carbs (33g total sugars)
10g fibre
