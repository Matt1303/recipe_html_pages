Spicy Tofu Tacos

★★★

Dinner, Fibre Fuelled, Lunches

Servings: 4 tacos

Ingredients

Cherry Tomato Salsa


45 grams sliced cherry tomatoes

0.5 jalapeñio, seeded and sliced

2 teaspoons garlic-infused olive oil

1 garlic clove, finely chopped

Juice of 0.5 lime (save the other half for serving)

Pinch salt

Pinch freshly ground black pepper


Spicy Tofu Taco Filling


85 grams black beans

0.25 teaspoon smoked paprika

0.25 teaspoon ground cumin

2 teaspoons fresh chopped chives

Dash cayenne pepper (optional)

0.25 teaspoon salt

0.25 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

1 teaspoon olive oil

170 grams firm tofu, drained, rinsed, and pressed

4 corn tortillas

10 grams fresh coriander

0.25 avocado, sliced

Description

Tacos for breakfast? Yes, please! Feel free to adjust the spice by decreasing the amount of jalapeño used in the salsa.

Directions

Make the salsa: In a small bowl, combine the sliced cherry tomatoes, jalapeño, olive oil, garlic, and lime juice with a pinch of salt and pepper. Set aside.

Make the taco filling: In a small saucepan, heat the black beans along with 2 tablespoons of water, 0.25 teaspoon smoked paprika, 0.5 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon chives, and a small pinch of cayenne, if using. Continue to cook for about 5 minutes, until warm. Gently smash the mixture using the back of a spoon, leaving some black beans whole. Set aside.

In a small bowl, add the remaining 0.5 teaspoon paprika, 0.5 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon chives, dash cayenne, if using, and salt and pepper. Add 2 tablespoons water and whisk together. Set aside.

Heat the oil in a medium frying pan over medium heat. Once hot, crumble the drained tofu into the pan along with the reserved taco sauce. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes, or until heated
through and the seasoning is absorbed.

To serve, warm the tortillas in a clean and dry frying pan until warm and flexible. Top with the tofu, black beans, fresh coriander, avocado, cherry tomato salsa, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Serve warm.
