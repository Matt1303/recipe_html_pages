Chicken Teriyaki, Rice and Broccoli

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 10 min Servings: Serves 4 Source: Realfood.tesco.com

Ingredients

3 tbsp light soy sauce

20g ginger, finely grated

2 garlic cloves, crushed

1 tbsp Dijon mustard

2 tbsp honey

4 chicken breasts or 500g diced pork

300g long-grain rice

Â½ tbsp vegetable oil

1 broccoli, cut into florets

2 tbsp crispy onions

Directions

Combine the soy, ginger, garlic, mustard and honey in a bowl. Add the chicken/pork and toss to coat. Leave to marinate for 15 mins at room temperature or for up to 24 hrs covered and chilled.

Cook the rice to pack instructions. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a nonstick pan over a high heat and fry the chicken/pork along with any marinade from the bowl. Stir fry for 10-12 mins until the chicken/pork is cooked through and the marinade is sticky and coats the pork.

Meanwhile, boil the broccoli in a pan of salted water for 6-8 mins until the stems are tender. Drain well.

Drain the rice and fluff up with a fork. Spoon into shallow bowls and top with the teriyaki chicken/pork, and the broccoli. Scatter over the crispy onions to serve.

Notes

Original recipe uses pork, we've replaced it with chicken
