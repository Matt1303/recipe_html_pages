Paprika & Sun-Dried Tomato Beans with Tofu Feta

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 30 min Servings: 2 Source: Mob.co.uk

Ingredients

For The Tofu Feta:

250 g Extra-firm Tofu

1½ tbsp Olive Oil

½ tbsp Dried Oregano

1 tsp Garlic Granules

2½ tbsp Nutritional Yeast

½ tsp Sea Salt

For The Beans:

½ Red Onion

Garlic Cloves

200 g Cherry Tomatoes

Sun Dried Tomatoes

1½ tsp Smoked Paprika

480 g Cooked Butter Beans

Reserved Butter Bean Liquid

30 g Fresh Basil

4 tbsp Nutritional Yeast

Olive Oil

Directions

For the tofu feta, crumble the tofu into a medium bowl and add the remaining ingredients. Stir well to combine. Season to taste and transfer to the fridge while you make the beans.

Dice the red onion, crush the garlic, halve the tomatoes and finely chop the sun-dried tomatoes.

Add a splash of olive oil to a large sauté pan. Once the oil is hot, add in the onion and fry over a medium heat with a small pinch of salt for 7-8 mins. Add the garlic and fry for 2-3 mins, then add the smoked paprika and fry for 1 min. Add the sundried tomatoes, stir, then add the cherry tomatoes and a pinch of sea salt. Cook for around 10 mins, stirring regularly, until you can squish them down into the pan to make a jammy sauce.

Pour two to three tablespoons of liquid out of the beans cans, then tip them into the pan with the remaining canning liquid. Stir and heat through, then add the basil and nutritional yeast. Cook over a medium heat for 5 mins, then season to taste.

Serve with the tofu feta and optional toppings.
