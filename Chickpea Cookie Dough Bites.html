Chickpea Cookie Dough Bites

★★★

Fibre Fuelled, Snacks

Ingredients

One 400 gram can chickpeas, drained, rinsed, and pat dry

35 grams oat flour

60 grams peanut butter

3 tablespoons 100% maple syrup, plus more as desired

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

0.25 teaspoon salt

0.25 teaspoon ground cinnamon

55 grams dairy-free chocolate chips

Description

Cookie dough, but it's healthy? Yep. We play to win the game. If you don't have oat flour, place 50 grams of jumbo oats in a blender or food processor and process until a fine flour forms.

Directions

Place the chickpeas, oat flour, peanut butter, maple syrup, vanilla salt, and cinnamon in the base of a food processor.

Process until a dough forms. Taste, adding in more maple syrup as desired. Fold in the chocolate chips, then pulse until just combined and the chips are incorporated.

Roll into balls and place on parchment paper-lined rimmed baking tray. Store in the fridge to harden for at least 15 minutes.

Transfer to an airtight container and keep in the fridge for up to 1 week.
