Jicama Fries

Dinner, Fibre Fuelled

Servings: 2

Ingredients

1 small jicama peeled and sliced into matchsticks

1.5 teaspoons olive oil

0.25 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon smoked paprika

0.5 teaspoon garlic powder

0.25 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Description

Jicama is a round root vegetable with a starchy inside that is popular in Mexican cuisine. Tossed with a few classic seasonings, it's our fun twist on baked french fries!

Directions

Preheat the oven to 220C. Line a rimmed baking tray with foil or parchment paper and set aside.

Place the sliced jicama in a large bowl and add the olive oil. Add in the salt, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper.

Toss until well coated and place in a single layer on the prepared baking tray.

Bake for 20 minutes, or until slightly crisp. Remove from the oven and serve.

Notes

Low FODMAP Options:

A 65 gram serving of fries is low FODMAP.
Omit the garlic powder if sensitive to fructans or garlic.
