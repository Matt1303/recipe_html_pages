Gingery Tomato Rice with Soy-Roasted Mushrooms

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: 30 min Servings: 4 Source: Mob.co.uk

Ingredients

800 g Mushrooms

1 tbsp Sesame Oil

3 tbsp Soy Sauce

1 tsp Ground White Pepper

Garlic Cloves

30 g Ginger

2 tbsp Tomato Purée

1 tsp Ground Turmeric

300 g Wholegrain Rice

800 g Tinned Chopped Tomatoes

1 L Vegetable Stock

Red Chilli

Spring Onions

100 g Spinach

15 g Fresh Coriander

Olive Oil

Directions

Heat the oven to 220°C. Slice or grate the mushrooms, then put them onto a baking tray with the sesame oil, soy sauce and a big pinch of ground white pepper. Mix well to coat and roast for 25 mins.

Meanwhile, finely chop the garlic then peel and finely chop the ginger. Heat a splash of olive oil in a large lidded saucepan set over a medium heat and add the garlic and ginger. Cook for 5 mins. Add the tomato purée and turmeric and cook for 2 mins until thickened. Add the rice, chopped tomatoes and stock, stir and pop a lid on. Cook for 30 mins until the rice is cooked but still a bit soupy. Throw in your spinach and give it a mix once it's looking ready.

Deseed and finely chop the chilli. Finely chop the spring onions and pick the coriander. Serve the rice in bowls topped with the mushrooms, spring onions, coriander and chilli.
