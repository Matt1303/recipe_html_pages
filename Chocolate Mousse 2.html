Chocolate Mousse

★★★

Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

400 gram block soft tofu, drained but not pressed

120 ml canned coconut milk

120 ml 100% maple syrup

50 grams unsweetened cocoa powder

2 tablespoons peanut butter

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 teaspoon salt

Description

Creamy, sweet and perfectly satisfying, this chocolate mousse is a plant protein- packed sweet treat.

Note: Place the can of coconut milk in the refrigerator upside down for at least 3 hours. This will allow the coconut milk fat/solids to harden and separate from the liquid. This helps make the mousse
creamier!

Directions

Place the tofu, coconut milk, maple syrup, cocoa powder, peanut butter, vanilla, and salt into the base of a food processor or high-powered blender. Puree until very smooth, scraping down the sides as necessary.

Divide among 4 to 6 ramekins or small bowls and allow to chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes or more to set.

Notes

Supercharge It!
Add up to 30 raspberries per serving for added sweet flavour, fibre, and another Plant Point.


Low FODMAP Options:
Silken tofu is high FODMAP, but firm tofu is not high FODMAP in a 165 gram serving size.
