Lentil-Sweet Potato Stew

★★★★

Dinner, Fibre Fuelled

Servings: 2

Ingredients

1 teaspoon garlic-infused olive oil

1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh ginger root

1 carrot, chopped

Salt and freshly ground black pepper

2 tablespoon dried or fresh chives (finely chopped if fresh)

1 sweet potato, peeled and chopped

2 teaspoons curry powder, plus more to taste

2.5 cups Biome Broth or vegetable stock

100 grams canned lentils

1 large handful fresh spinach or kale leaves

Description

Garlic-infused olive oil and fresh ginger root really make the curry stand out! Make a batch of this stew on the weekend and reheat for lunches or dinner during a busy week.

Directions

Heat a medium pan over medium heat. Add the olive oil, ginger root, and chopped carrots. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper, then cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the chives and sweet potato and cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in the curry powder.

Add the stock to the pan, cover, and raise the heat to medium-high to bring to a low boil. Add the lentils to the stew and stir to
combine. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, uncovered, for about 20 minutes, until the potatoes are fork tender.

Add salt, pepper, or curry powder to taste. Just before serving, stir in the greens until just wilted. Serve with a side of sourdough bread, if preferred.

Notes

Make-Ahead Tips:

Chop the sweet potato and carrot ahead of time.


Supercharge It!

Top with fresh spring onions and parsley.
