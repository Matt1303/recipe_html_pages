Creamy Coriander Salad

★★★★

Fibre Fuelled, Side Salad

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4

Ingredients

Creamy Coriander Sauce


1 jalapeño, roughly chopped

Half a bunch coriander

20 grams slivered almonds

Zest and juice of 1 lime

1 teaspoon salt


For Salad


300 grams shredded lettuce

100 grams chopped tomatoes

50 grams sliced black olives

2 sliced spring onions

10 grams chopped coriander

Description

Originally used for tempeh tacos.

Directions

Make the creamy coriander sauce: Place the jalapeñio, coriander, almonds, lime zest, lime juice, 120 ml water, and the salt into a blender and puree until creamy and smooth. The consistency should be similar to a thick salad dressing. Add more water, one tablespoon at a time, if the dressing is too thick.

Assemble the salad: Toss together the lettuce, tomatoes, olives, spring onions and coriander and divide into two bowls.

Top with any desired filling (originally tempeh tacos) and drizzle with the
coriander dressing.

Notes

Make-Ahead Tip:

Prep the creamy coriander sauce ahead of time, up to 3 days before serving. Portion half of the [tempeh taco filling and any]
coriander dressing to use later in the week.
