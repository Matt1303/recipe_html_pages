Kimchi Peanut Butter Toast

Cook Time: 5 min Servings: 2 Source: Mob.co.uk

Ingredients

Slices Sourdough

80 g Vegan Kimchi

3 tbsp Crunchy Peanut Butter

1 tbsp Sesame Seeds

Spring Onion

1 tsp Tahini

Â½ tsp Agave

Directions

Toast your bread. Finely slice the spring onion greens.

Spread the peanut butter thickly over the toast. Squeeze over the agave and a sprinkle of salt, if you like. Pile on the kimchi and finish with the sesame seeds, tahini and spring onion if using.
