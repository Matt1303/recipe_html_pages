Down 'N' Dirty Kale Salad

★★★

Fibre Fuelled, Side Salad

Ingredients

70+ grams kale, tough stems removed and chopped

3 teaspoons low-sodium tamari

30 grams spinach

2 tablespoons chives

0.25 teaspoon salt

0.125 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

0.25 avocado, mashed

2 tablespoons almond butter

0.5 celery stick, chopped

2 tablespoons walnuts, roughly chopped (optional)

50 grams chopped spring onions (option)

Description

This is one of our favourite ways to enjoy kale! The avocado-almond
butter dressing is creamy and bursting with flavour. Simple to make,
filled with flavour and texture, and brimming with plant-powered nutrients. We use this as a side dish for multiple meals throughout the plan.

Directions

In a medium bowl, drizzle the kale with 1 teaspoon tamari and massage with your hands to soften and break down the kale. Add the spinach, chives, salt, and pepper and toss together once more.

In a separate small bowl, mash the avocado with the almond butter and whisk in the remaining 2 teaspoons tamari to thin.

Pour the avocado mixture over the kale and combine thoroughly with your hands or with tongs, massaging to coat every piece
Add the celery, walnuts, and spring onions and serve.
