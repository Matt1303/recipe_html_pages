Creamy Chai Oats

★★★

Breakfasts, Fibre Fuelled

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2

Ingredients

1 chai tea bag

360 ml unsweetened almond milk

0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon

0.25 teaspoon ground cardamom

0.25 teaspoon ground nutmeg

100 grams jumbo oats

1 teaspoon ground flaxseeds

1 teaspoon chia seeds

1 tablespoon hemp seeds

1 banana, mashed

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Pinch salt

1 teaspoon 100% maple syrup (optional)

Description

All the flavours of spicy, comforting chai in your morning bowl of creamy porridge.

Directions

In a medium saucepan, bring the chai tea bag, 120 ml water, almond milk, cinnamon, cardamom, and nutmeg to a boil over medium heat. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Remove the tea bag and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Stir in the oats, flax, chia, and hemp seeds. Reduce the heat to
medium-low and cook for about 3 minutes, until the oats are thick.

Stir in the mashed banana and cook for one
more minute. Remove from the heat, stir in the vanilla and salt, then cover and allow to
finish cooking for 5 minutes. Stir in the maple syrup, if using, and serve.

Notes

Supercharge It!
Top this porridge with your favourite berries for extra sweetness and Plant Points.

Make-Ahead Tips:
You can make this abead of time and store in an airtight container in the fridge. Enjoy warm or cold.
