Supercharged Roasted Roots

★★★

Fibre Fuelled, Part Of Other Recipe

Servings: 4

Ingredients

260 grams cubed sweet potato

2 parsnips, chopped

6 large radishes, chopped

1 tablespoon olive oil, or garlic-infused olive oil (see page 246)

2 tablespoons vegetable stock

0.5 teaspoon salt

0.5 teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Description

This topping is designed to supercharge your meals wherever you want a little fibre boost. Add a handful to salads, Nourishing Buddha Bowls (page 295), or Tofu Scramble Bowls (page 286), of just enjoy them on their own.

This mix is perfect for Week 1, but the technigue can be used on almost any vegetable out there, though cooking times may need to be adjusted.

Directions

Preheat the oven to 220C.

Toss the sweet potato, parsnips, radishes, olive oil, stock, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until well combined. Place on
a rimmed baking tray or roasting tin in a single layer (you may need use two, depending on size) and cover with foil.

Roast for 35 minutes, or until mostly soft. Remove from the oven, remove the foil, and stir. Return to the oven for 10 more minutes, or until the edges become lightly crisp.
