Chickpea Meatballs

★★

Dinner, Fibre Fuelled

Difficulty: Medium Servings: Makes 18 meatballs

Ingredients

1.5 tablespoons ground flaxseed

500 grams canned chickpeas. drained and rinsed (85 gram serving

yellow light,GOS)

85 grams walnuts

90 grams bread crumbs, plus more as needed

3 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for drizzling

1.5 teaspoons dried oregano

1.5 teaspoons dried basil

1.5 teaspoons dried parsley

0.75 teaspoon salt

Freshly ground black pepper


Pasta Marinara


170 to 225 grams dried pasta of your choice

480 ml marinara sauce

6 Chickpea Meatballs

Pumpkin Seed Parmesan (page 301; optional)


Sandwich


240 ml marinara sauce

6 Chickpea Meatballs

2 submarine rolls

Description

You'll meal-prep these meatballs to be used with pasta, marinara sauce, and quick garlic broccoli for dinner one night, then tucked
into a submarine roll with the remaining pasta sauce for lunch the next day.
Any leftovers can be individually frozen, then stored in the freezer. Note that these will hold their shape when warmed through
but may start to crumble if placed in sauce for a long period of time.

Directions

Preheat the oven to 230°C. Lightly oil a rimmed baking tray or line with parchment paper and set aside.

Mix together 60 ml water and the ground flaxseeds in a bowl and set aside to gel. Place the chickpeas and walnuts in the base of a food processor and pulse until very finely chopped.

Remove the chickpea mixture from the food processor and place in a large bowl. Add the flaxseed mixture, bread crumbs, olive oil,
oregano, basil, and parsley, Stir together until the mixture is combined. It should stick together easily. If it's too sticky, add in
more bread crumbs. If it's too dry, add a little more oil or water. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Scoop out 1 tablespoon of the mixture at a time, roll into a ball, and place on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with more olive oil for crispier meatballs, then bake for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.

To serve with pasta, cook the pasta according to the packet instructions. Warm the marinara sauce in a medium saucepan and right before serving, stir together with the meatballs. Serve with the cooked pasta and Pumpkin Seed Parmesan, if desired.

To serve as a sandwich, warm the sauce and the meatballs, then toss to just combine. Stuff into a toasted (or untoasted) submarine roll and enjoy!
