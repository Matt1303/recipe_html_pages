Zesty Lemon Chia Pudding

Desserts, Fibre Fuelled

Difficulty: Medium Servings: Makes about 500ml

Ingredients

240 ml unsweetened almond milk

1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest

60 ml freshly squeezed lemon juice

1 to 2 tablespoons 100% maple syrup

0.25 teaspoon ground turmeric

Pinch salt

40 grams chia seeds

Description

This lemon chia pudding is a Fibre Fuelled riff on lemon pudding. Who needs dairy and egg yolks when you have chia seeds? Chia
seeds swell when mixed with liquid, creating a thick, creamy pudding out of lemon juice and almond milk. For a creamier pudding, swap in canned coconut milk after Week 3.

Directions

In a medium bowl, whisk together the almond milk, lemon zest, lemon juice, maple syrup, turmeric, and salt. Add the chia sed
and whisk until very well combined, then place in the fridge for 15 minutes. Remove, whisk together again, then cover and place in the fridge to gel for at least 2 hours, or overnight.

Place in an airtight container and keep in the fridge for up to 7 days.

Notes

Make-Ahead Tip:
Make the chia pudding abead of time. It will keep for up to 1 week in the fridge.

Supercharge lt!
Top with berries and up to 35 grams unsweetened shredded coconut.
After Week 2, you can add Whipped Coconut Cream, too.
