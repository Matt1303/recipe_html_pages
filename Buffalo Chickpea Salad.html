Buffalo Chickpea Salad

★★★

Fibre Fuelled, Lunches, Side Salad

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2

Ingredients

Sweet Hummus Dressing


60 grams Pumpkin Hummus (separate recipe) or other hummus

2 teaspoons 100% maple syrup

2 tablespoons red wine vinegar or freshly squeezed lemon juice

2 teaspoons hot water


Buffalo Chickpeas


85 grams canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed

2 teaspoons garlic-infused olive oil

1 tablespoon hot sauce

0.25 teaspoon finely chopped garlic

Pinch salt


Salad

280 grams chopped cos lettuce

0.25 avocado, sliced

45 grams halved cherry tomatoes

Description

We simply had to have this flavour in the four-week plan. Spicy buffalo chickpeas mixed with sweet hummus dressing A match made in heaven!

Directions

Make the dressing: Whisk together the hummus, maple syrup, and vinegar until creamy and smooth. Keep whisking while slowly adding in the hot water. The dressing should be thick but pourable. Add more water as needed.

Make the chickpeas: Toss the chickpeas with the infused olive oil, hot sauce, garlic, and salt. Heat a frying pan over medium heat. Add the chickpeas and sauté for 3 minutes, or until the chickpeas start to dry out and are heated through. Use the back of a spoon to gently mash some of the chickpeas together, keeping most of them whole. Remove from the heat and set aside.

Make the salad: Toss the cos lettuce with the dressing, then gently toss in the avocado, tomatoes, and buffalo chickpeas. Divide between two bowls and serve immediately.

Notes

Low FODMAP Options:
Use the FODMAP-friendly Pumpkin Hummus (separate recipe) in this recipe as most prepared hummus will contain garlic and/or onion.
Limit chickpeas to a single 40 gram serving per person.
Garlic is high in fructans. Substitute 1 teaspoon chives.


Make-Ahead Option:
Prepare the Sweet Hummus Dressing and Buffalo Chickpeas abead of time.
Before serving, warm the chickpeas, then assemble as directed.
