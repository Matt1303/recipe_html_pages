Peanut & Cucumber Ramen Noodle Salad

Prep Time: 3 min Cook Time: 7 min Servings: 2 Source: Mob.co.uk

Ingredients

200 g Instant Noodles

Cucumber

100 g Frozen Edamame

20 g Fresh Coriander

10 g Fresh Mint

20 g Roasted Unsalted Peanuts

Spring Onion

2 tbsp Vegan Kimchi

For The Peanut Sauce:

2 tbsp Peanut Butter

1 tbsp Sesame Oil

2 tbsp Soy Sauce

2 tbsp Water

Lime

½ tsp Gochujang

½ tsp Agave

1 tsp Fresh Ginger

Directions

Put the edamame in a heatproof bowl and cover with hot water to defrost. Meanwhile, cook the noodles following pack instructions. Rinse the cooked noodles under cold water and set aside.

For the sauce, peel and mince the ginger and transfer to a small bowl. Add the soy sauce, peanut butter, sesame oil, lime juice, agave, water and gochujang, then whisk to combine. Set aside.

Slice the cucumber in half lengthwise and scoop out the watery seeds with a spoon (you can drain the water out of this and drink it, if you like!). Slice the cucumber into 0.5cm diagonal slices and transfer to a large bowl.

Roughly chop the coriander leaves and finely chop the stems. Pick the mint leaves. Roughly chop the peanuts. Finely slice the spring onion.

Add all of the herbs to the cucumber bowl along with the edamame, spring onion, dressing and noodles. Toss well.

Finish with a drizzle of sesame oil and top with the peanuts and kimchi.
