Supercharged Miso Soup

★★★★

Fibre Fuelled, Lunches

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2

Ingredients

60 grams soba noodles

480 ml Biome Broth (separate recipe)

1 tablespoon wakame seaweed

45 grams white miso paste

15 grams chopped baby spinach

50 grams chopped spring onions

60 grams firm tofu, drained and cubed

10 grams dried shiitake mushrooms, rehydrated

Description

Miso is a paste made from fermented soybeans and very common in Asian cuisine. This soup is warm, comforting, and delicious.

Directions

Bring a medium pot of water to a boil over medium-high heat. Cook the soba noodles according to the packet instructions, then drain and rinse with cool water to stop the cooking. Set aside.

In the same pot, bring 480 ml of cold water and the stock to a boil over medium-high heat. Add the wakame, reduce the heat, and simmer over low heat for about 5 minutes.

In a small bowl, combine the miso with 1 to 2 teaspoons warm water to thin it out to the consistency of a thick, creamy sauce. Set aside.

Add the baby spinach, spring onions, tofu, and shiitake mushrooms to the stock. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then cover, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 5 minutes.

Remove from the heat and stir in the miso paste and cooked soba noodles. Serve.

Notes

Supercharge Options:
Top with sesame seeds and/or sliced green onions for an added texture bonus and flavour boost.
