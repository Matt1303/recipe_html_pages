Pumpkin Hummus

★★★★

Fibre Fuelled, Part Of Other Recipe

Servings: Makes about 480 grams

Ingredients

One 400 gram tin chickpeas, drained and rinsed

160 grams pumpkin puree

0.5 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon ground cumin

Juice of 1 large lemon

1 tablespoon garlic-infused olive oil

Description

Pumpkin is a fantastic source of fibre, vitamin A, and antioxidants. This savoury pumpkin dip tastes wonderful with fresh vegetables, wholegrain sourdough toast, or any other way you can think to use it!

Directions

Place the chickpeas, pumpkin, salt, cumin, lemon juice, and olive oil in the base of a food processor. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.

Transfer to an airtight container and store in the fridge for up to 1 week.

Lower FODMAP Options:
Enjoy a smaller, 2-tablespoon serving of the hummus for a lower FODMAP option.

Supercharge It!
